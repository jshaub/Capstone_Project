Overview

    How to use:

  1. Sign-up (If you have an account, then you just login right away)
    1. Enter your PSU E-mail
    2. Enter your own password
    3. Hit "Create Account"
    4. You will be sent a verification e-mail to confirm it's you
    5. Click the link in the e-mail and then you're done!

  2. Explore
    1. Enter your interests
    2. Our machine learning algorithm will match you based on your interests and give a list of recommended friends
    3. You choose whether to add or reject potential friends
    4. If you reject a person, then you move on to the next recommendation
    5. If you accept a potential friend, then all you have to do is just talk to them and you're done!
